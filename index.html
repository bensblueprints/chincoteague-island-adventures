Meet Captain Randy — Your Guide to Chincoteague's Wild Side

“Every trip on the water is different. The ponies, the dolphins, the sunsets — I've been doing this for decades and it still takes my breath away.”

Captain Randy has spent a lifetime on the waters of Chincoteague and Assateague Islands. Born and raised on Virginia's Eastern Shore, he knows every channel, every hidden cove, and every spot where the wild ponies gather at dawn.

Whether you're here for a peaceful nature cruise, the excitement of the Pony Swim, or a guided waterfowl hunt, Captain Randy brings unmatched local knowledge and a genuine love for sharing this island's magic with visitors from all over the world.
